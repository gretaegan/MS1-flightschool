About Us

Based out of Westing Airfield on the outskirts of Dublin, we are Ireland's oldest flight school, training pilots since 1940. The Academy is comprised of a terminal building, 4 aircraft hangars, a 1500m long paved tarmac runway and our training facility. Our full-motion Boeing 737-800 simulator is located on site in hangar 3. Our team of Instructors are all ex- commercial and military pilots with a wealth of experience.
