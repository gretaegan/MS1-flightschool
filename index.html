About Us

Based out of Westing Airfield on the outskirts of Dublin, we are Ireland's oldest flight school, training pilots since 1940. The Academy is comprised of a terminal building, 4 aircraft hangars, a 1500m paved tarmac runway and our training facility. Our full-motion Boeing 737-800 simulator is located on site in hangar 3. Our team of Instructors are all ex- commercial and military pilots with a wealth of experience.

Entry Requirements for Integrated Course 2021 :

Due to the intensity and fast pace of the course, prospective students must have the following:

  • Be at least 18 years of age prior to the starting date
  • Be in possession of a Class 1 Medical Certificate
  • Be proficient in mathematics and physics

If entry requirements are met you will be contacted by phone or email to arrange an assessment and interview at our training facility.

Integrated Flight Training

What is it?

The Integrated style of flight training is the most popular way to become a Commercial Airline pilot. Cadets will go from zero flight experience to airline cockpit ready in a timeframe of 24-36months. The course consists of 290 hours of flying time, 6 months of classroom learning and 14 written examinations.
