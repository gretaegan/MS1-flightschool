About Us

Established in 1967 and approved by the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA), we are Ireland's oldest flight school. Our facilities consist of a terminal building and ATC tower, a 1500m paved tarmac runway with full approach light system, 4 aircraft hangars and our pilot Academy. Our team of Instructors are ex-military and ex-commercial Captains and First Officers with decades of experience. Our fleet includes single-engine Cessna 172s, twin-engine Be76s and a full motion Boeing 737-800 simulator certified within EASA standards.

Integrated Flight Training

What is it?

The Integrated style of flight training is the most popular and favourable way to become a Commercial Airline pilot. Cadets will go from zero flight experience to airline cockpit ready in a timeframe of 24-36months. The course consists of 290 hours of flying time, 6 months of classroom learning and 14 written examinations. Pricing includes iPads and all learning materials needed for the duration of the course.

Entry requirements for 2021

Due to the intensity and fast pace of the course, prospective students must have the following

  • Be at least 18 years of age prior to the starting date
  • Be in possession of a Class 1 Medical Certificate
  • Be proficient in mathematics and physics

If entry requirements are met you will be contacted by phone or email to arrange an assessment and interview at our training facility.
